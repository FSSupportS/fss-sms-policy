UGI Utilities FSS – SMS Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

UGI Utilities – Field System Support (FSS)

UGI Utilities operates an internal SMS support notification program through its Field System Support (FSS) team. This page describes how SMS messaging is used, how recipients can manage their preferences, and the terms governing this program.

Program Description

SMS messages are sent by the UGI Utilities Field System Support team for the purpose of internal operational support, including helpdesk communications and field technician notifications.

How to Opt In

Recipients are enrolled through direct communication with the FSS support team or by texting START to the FSS support line at 610-736-5346.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on support activity and operational needs.

Fees

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

HELP

Reply HELP for assistance, or contact the FSS team directly at FieldSystemSupportGroup@ugi.com or 610-736-5346.

Data Privacy

No personal information collected through this SMS program is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes. SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers provided for SMS are not shared with third parties or affiliates.

SMS Terms of Service

By texting UGI Utilities FSS or by opting in through direct communication with the FSS team, you agree to receive internal IT support messages from UGI Utilities FSS. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to opt out. Reply HELP for assistance. Visit https://fssupports.github.io/fss-sms-policy/ for our full privacy policy and terms of service.
