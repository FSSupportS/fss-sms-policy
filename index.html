UGI Utilities FSS – SMS Privacy Policy

UGI Utilities – Field System Support (FSS)

UGI Utilities operates an internal SMS support notification program through its Field System Support (FSS) team. This page describes how SMS messaging is used and how recipients can manage their preferences.

Program Description

SMS messages are sent by the UGI Utilities Field System Support team for the purpose of internal operational support, including support communications and field technician notifications.

How to Opt In

Recipients are enrolled through direct communication with the FSS support team or by texting START to the FSS support line.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on support activity and operational needs.

Fees

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out

Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe. You will receive one confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

HELP

Reply HELP for assistance, or contact the FSS team directly at FieldSystemSupportGroup@ugi.com or 610-736-5346.

Data Privacy

No personal information collected through this SMS program is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties for marketing purposes.
